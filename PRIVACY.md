# Privacy Policy - TrueLinks

TrueLinks does not collect, store, or transmit personal data.
* No user activity is tracked
* No browsing history is recorded
* No data is sold or shared with third parties

Link analysis is performed locally whenever possible.

If you have questions or concerns, you may contact the developer at:
truelinks.dev@gmail.com

This policy may be updated if the extension's functionality changes.
